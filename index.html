We are Vae Victis, a Robotics team from George Mason High School in Falls Church, VA. Founded in 2004, we participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). Our team has maintained a high place in FRC competitions by having an active and engaged student body of about 35 students and mentors who work on robots on and off season. We attend competitions, volunteer, take part in community events, build robots, and have lots of fun. The team is comprised of high school students of all ages who are all very committed to the team.

In the 2016 season, we took first place in the Greater DC District competition, the Bethesda District Competition, and the FIRST Chesapeake district championship, qualifying us for the world championship. After a hard fight at worlds, we made it to the division semifinals in the 2016 Newton Division, widely believed to have been the strongest group of teams in FIRST history.

We are excited for the 2017 season to start! If you'd like to help us with fundraising, please visit this page.
